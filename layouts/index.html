I am honoured and grateful to be selected as a part of the 2023 Astra Fellowship cohort. This is a site to document my experience over the course of the year.

fellowship jam series

Over the first two months of the program, we will be focussing on experimentation through game jams.

Over this period, my goal is to improve at the craft of creating and designing games. This has two aspects.
  • get better at identifying the specific experience that I would like to deliver via the game, and then execute on that.
  • get more experience with exploring the systems that I create, and finding the interesting points of emergence within them.
